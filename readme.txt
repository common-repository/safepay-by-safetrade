== Description ==
The SafePay plugin allows merchants to use the SafeTrade escrow service within their e-commerce sites.
SafePay protects your customers and sellers interest while shopping and selling on your e-commerce platform.

###Get your money back ######

SafePay ensures that your customers get their money back if they get a wrong or damaged item. Ts & Cs apply


###Handle payments######

SafePay offers your customers a wide variety of payment methods from debit & credit cards to internet banking payment and even from a  bank branch


###Payout######

SafePay handles payout to your merchant/seller bank account or to your bank account. Payout is made 24 hours after concluding a transaction.


###API######

We have developed an API that would allow you to directly interact with out platform and get the necessary information related to your business.


###No more transfer charges######

With SafePay handling all monetary aspects of your e-commerce transactions you need not to worry about transfer charges. All you have to do is collect what is yours.


###No corporate account needed######

With SafePay you don't need to own a company account to receive payments. We pay to current and savings account with every bank in Nigeria.
                        

== Installation ==
1. Upload the plugin folder 'safepay.zip' to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. The plugin admin options can be located in Woocommerce > Settings > Checkout > SafePay
